We surveyed small and mid-size business owners about AI automation.

Not vendors. Not consultants. The people actually running businesses.

Here's what stood out:

## 52% Are Exploring AI—But Don't Know Where to Start

They've heard the noise. They know something is changing. But the landscape is overwhelming and most "AI solutions" feel like they were built for Fortune 500 companies with dedicated IT teams.

## 44% Said Their #1 Goal Is Saving Time

Not cutting costs. Not replacing people. Saving time.

They're spending 10+ hours a week on tasks that should be automated: follow-ups, scheduling, reporting, data entry. They know it. They just don't have a clear path to fix it.

## The Real Gap: Clarity, Not Awareness

What surprised us most: The gap isn't awareness. Business owners know AI is relevant.

The gap is clarity.

They don't need another tool with 200 features. They need someone to look at their specific business and say: "Here's what's costing you time. Here's what to automate first. Here's what it looks like."
