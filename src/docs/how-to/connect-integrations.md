# Connect Integrations

Link your favorite tools to Sparkwave for seamless automation.

## Overview

Integrations allow Sparkwave to work with your existing business tools. Connect your CRM, email provider, calendar, and more to create powerful automated workflows.

## Available Integrations

### Communication
- **Email** - Gmail, Outlook, custom SMTP
- **SMS** - Twilio, built-in messaging
- **Chat** - Slack, Discord

### Scheduling
- **Google Calendar** - Sync appointments and events
- **Calendly** - Booking management
- **Wix Bookings** - Service scheduling

### CRM & Contacts
- **Native CRM** - Built-in contact management
- **Wix Contacts** - Import from Wix sites
- **CSV Import** - Bulk contact upload

### Website
- **Wix** - Full site integration
- **Webhooks** - Connect any service

## How to Connect an Integration

### Step 1: Open Settings

1. Click your profile icon in the top-right
2. Select **Settings**
3. Navigate to the **Integrations** tab

### Step 2: Choose Your Integration

1. Browse available integrations
2. Click **Connect** on your chosen service
3. Follow the authorization prompts

### Step 3: Configure Permissions

Most integrations require specific permissions:

- **Read access** - View data from the service
- **Write access** - Create or update records
- **Full access** - Complete control

> **Tip:** Grant only the permissions you need for your automations.

### Step 4: Test the Connection

After connecting:

1. Click **Test Connection**
2. Verify data is syncing correctly
3. Check for any error messages

## Managing Integrations

### View Connected Services

Go to **Settings > Integrations** to see all active connections with their status and last sync time.

### Disconnect an Integration

1. Find the integration in your list
2. Click the **⋮** menu
3. Select **Disconnect**
4. Confirm the action

> **Note:** Disconnecting won't delete your data, but automations using that integration will stop working.

### Refresh Credentials

Some integrations require periodic re-authorization:

1. You'll receive a notification when credentials expire
2. Click **Refresh** to re-authorize
3. Follow the prompts to update access

## Troubleshooting

### Connection Failed

- Check your internet connection
- Verify your credentials are correct
- Ensure the third-party service is operational
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting

### Data Not Syncing

- Check integration permissions
- Verify the sync schedule
- Look for errors in the activity log
- Contact support if issues persist

## Best Practices

1. **Start with essentials** - Connect your most-used tools first
2. **Review permissions** - Only grant necessary access
3. **Monitor syncs** - Check the activity log regularly
4. **Keep credentials updated** - Refresh before they expire
