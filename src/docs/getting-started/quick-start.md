# Quick Start Guide

Get Sparkwave up and running in just a few minutes. This guide walks you through the essential setup steps.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, you'll need:

- A Sparkwave account (sign up at [sparkwaveai.app](https://sparkwaveai.app))
- Admin access to your business tools (Wix, payment processor, etc.)
- 10-15 minutes of uninterrupted time

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to [sparkwaveai.app/auth](https://sparkwaveai.app/auth)
2. Click **Sign Up** and enter your email
3. Verify your email address
4. You'll be redirected to the onboarding flow

## Step 2: Complete Onboarding

The onboarding wizard will guide you through:

### Business Profile
- Enter your business name
- Select your industry
- Set your timezone

### Connect Integrations
- Link your Wix account (if applicable)
- Connect payment processing
- Set up email/SMS channels

### Configure Your First Agent
- Choose an agent to activate (we recommend starting with Lead Follow-up)
- Review default settings
- Customize messaging if desired

## Step 3: Verify Everything Works

After onboarding, you'll land on **Mission Control**. Here's how to verify setup:

1. **Check Agent Status** — Look at the sidebar. Agents should show green (healthy) status.
2. **Review Health Dashboard** — The health monitoring section should show all systems operational.
3. **Test Rico** — Click the chat button and ask "What's my status?" to verify Rico is working.

## Step 4: Your First Automation

Let's test that everything is connected:

1. Go to **Contacts** and add a test contact (use your own phone/email)
2. Tag the contact as a "New Lead"
3. Watch the Activity feed in Mission Control
4. Within minutes, you should see the Lead Follow-up agent pick up the contact

🎉 **Congratulations!** Your first automation is live.
