# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is Sparkwave?

Sparkwave is an AI-powered business automation platform that helps small and medium businesses automate their operations, from customer communications to task management.

### How does Sparkwave AI work?

Sparkwave uses advanced AI agents that can understand your business context and automate repetitive tasks. These agents learn from your patterns and preferences to provide increasingly personalized assistance.

### Is my data secure?

Yes. Sparkwave uses enterprise-grade encryption for all data in transit and at rest. We follow industry best practices for security and comply with major data protection regulations.

### Can I use Sparkwave on mobile?

Yes! Sparkwave is fully responsive and works on any device. Access your dashboard, chat with Rico, and manage your business from anywhere.

## Pricing & Plans

### Is there a free trial?

Yes, Sparkwave offers a free trial period so you can explore the platform before committing.

### Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Absolutely. You can change your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and support annual billing for additional savings.

## Technical Questions

### What integrations does Sparkwave support?

Sparkwave integrates with popular business tools including:
- CRM systems
- Email marketing platforms
- Calendar applications
- Communication tools

See our [Integrations Guide](/docs/how-to/connect-integrations) for the full list.

### Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export your data at any time from the Settings menu. We support common formats like CSV and JSON.

### What browsers are supported?

Sparkwave works best on:
- Chrome (recommended)
- Firefox
- Safari
- Edge

We recommend keeping your browser updated to the latest version for the best experience.
