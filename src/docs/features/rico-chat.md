# Rico AI Assistant

Rico is your personal AI assistant built into Sparkwave. Ask questions, get status updates, run reports, and troubleshoot issues—all through natural conversation.

## What is Rico?

Rico is an AI assistant that understands your business and your Sparkwave setup. Unlike generic chatbots, Rico:

- **Knows your data** — Has context about your contacts, agents, and activity
- **Takes action** — Can run reports, check status, and trigger tasks
- **Learns your preferences** — Adapts to how you work over time
- **Available 24/7** — Instant help whenever you need it

## Accessing Rico

### Chat Button

Click the chat bubble icon (💬) in the bottom-right corner of any Sparkwave page.

### Keyboard Shortcut

Press `Cmd/Ctrl + K` to open Rico from anywhere.

### Voice (Coming Soon)

Speak to Rico hands-free for quick queries.

## Example Conversations

### Checking Status

**Rico:** "Good morning! Here's your daily snapshot:

📊 **Today's Activity:**
- 47 tasks completed (98% success rate)
- 12 new leads followed up
- 8 booking reminders sent
- 3 review requests delivered

🟢 **All Systems Healthy**
Your agents are running smoothly. No errors in the last 24 hours.

Anything specific you'd like to dive into?"

---

### Troubleshooting

**You:** "My booking reminders aren't going out"

**Rico:** "Let me check that for you...

I found the issue! Your SMS integration is showing a 'Rate Limited' status—looks like you hit the daily sending limit.

**Quick fixes:**
1. Wait until the limit resets (midnight UTC)
2. Upgrade your SMS plan for higher limits
3. Adjust reminder timing to spread sends throughout the day

Would you like me to help adjust the reminder schedule?"

## Tips for Better Conversations

### Be Specific

Instead of: "Show me stuff"
Try: "Show me failed tasks from this week"

### Use Natural Language

Rico understands casual speech:
- "What's up with my agents?"
- "Anything I should worry about?"
- "Give me the highlights"

### Ask Follow-ups

Rico remembers context within a conversation:
- "Tell me more about that"
- "Can you explain that error?"
- "What should I do about it?"

### Request Actions

Rico can do things, not just answer:
- "Send me that report by email"
- "Pause the review agent"
- "Add a note to John's contact"

## Rico's Capabilities

### What Rico CAN Do

✅ Answer questions about your Sparkwave setup
✅ Run and export reports
✅ Check system status and health
✅ Explain errors and suggest fixes
✅ Guide you through features
✅ Look up contact and task information

### What Rico CANNOT Do

❌ Access data outside Sparkwave
❌ Make changes to your integrations directly
❌ Send messages on your behalf without confirmation
❌ Access other users' accounts

## Privacy & Security

- Rico only sees data you have access to
- Conversations aren't shared with other users
- Chat history can be cleared at any time
- No data is used to train AI models

## Troubleshooting Rico

### Rico Not Responding

1. Check your internet connection
2. Try refreshing the page
3. Clear browser cache
4. Check Sparkwave status page

### Wrong Information

Rico can occasionally misunderstand. If you get incorrect info:
1. Rephrase your question
2. Provide more context
3. Report the issue (helps us improve!)

### Can't Complete Action

If Rico says it can't do something:
1. It may require manual navigation
2. You might need different permissions
3. The feature may not be available yet
