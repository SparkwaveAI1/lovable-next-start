## Buyer-facing demo story

### One-line promise
Sparkwave helps high-value service businesses stop losing leads by responding faster, following up consistently, and showing the owner what needs attention.

### 30-second demo opening
"Most businesses do not lose leads because the lead was bad. They lose them because the first response was slow, the follow-up was inconsistent, or nobody could see which opportunities were slipping. This AI Growth Hub gives your team one place to see new inquiries, what happened next, which leads need attention today, and what the system recommends doing next."

### Simple product explanation
"We install an AI Growth Hub for your business. It watches new leads, helps follow up, shows stale opportunities, tracks what is running, and gives you a daily Business Brain report with the next things to fix or act on."

### Do not say in the demo
- agentic architecture
- learning layer
- context ingestion
- semantic monitoring
- organizational intelligence graph
- autonomous CRM migration
- guaranteed revenue, CAC, CPA, or booked-call claims

### Say instead
- stop losing leads
- respond faster
- follow up automatically
- see what needs attention
- know which leads are stale
- see what the system noticed today
- get simple next steps

## Recommended demo flow

1. Start with the leak
   - "Here are the new leads, how fast they were answered, who booked, and who is going stale."

2. Show the immediate response path
   - "When a lead comes in, this is what happens first, what message they receive, and when a human should step in."

3. Show follow-up and recovery
   - "The system keeps track of who needs a nudge, who missed an appointment, and who should be re-engaged."

4. Show the Business Brain report
   - "This is the daily snapshot: what changed, what is slipping, what customers are asking about, and what to do next."

5. End with the Growth Agent
   - "You can ask your Growth Agent plain questions like: Which leads need follow-up today? What should we post about this week? Where are we losing people?"

6. Optional expansion preview
   - "Once the lead response system is working, we can add content, SEO, reviews, campaigns, and reporting around the same hub."

## Universal module set

Use the same underlying modules across demos, with labels changed by vertical.

1. Lead Inbox
   - Purpose: show new inquiries, source, status, response speed, owner, and next action.

2. Fast Response
   - Purpose: show the first response and handoff path after someone inquires.

3. Follow-Up Tracker
   - Purpose: show follow-up stage, stale leads, no-reply leads, and recovery opportunities.

4. Booking / Revenue Opportunities
   - Purpose: show booked, not booked, quoted, pending, and high-value opportunities.

5. Automations Running
   - Purpose: show what the system is doing without exposing technical workflow details.

6. Business Brain Daily Report
   - Purpose: show what the system noticed, what is slipping, and recommended next actions.

7. Ask Your Growth Agent
   - Purpose: simple chat-style interface for questions about leads, follow-up, content, and reports.

8. Content Ideas Preview
   - Purpose: small expansion preview, not the main event.
